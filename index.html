Supercharge your journey

Enjoy the ride with premium features such as the heated grips, full-color instrumentation, LED cornering lamps, large windshield, DC outlet plus special color and graphics. Aggressive body design, specially machined wheels and all-LED lighting are among the styling highlights that make the Ninja H2 SX SE stand apart as an icon of sport performance, luxury and style. This is where luxury and performance reach stunning new heights.
